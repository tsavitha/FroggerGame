Rules of the Frogger Game

The game surface has 2 rows of grass, 3 rows of stones and a row of water.
There are bugs running across the stone rows that are the enemies and a stone on one of the rows that the player has to avoid.
There are also gems for extra points -
  • Orange - 5 pts
  • Blue - 15 pts
  • Green - 25 pts
  • Star - 50 pts
The player starts with 3 lives.
The aim of the game is for the player to start on the grass rows, move one square at a time across the stones and get to the water without being hit by the bugs or hitting a stone.
The player looses a life everytime he hits a rock or bug. When the player looses all 3 lives, he is prompted either to start a new game or quit.
The player gets 200 points everytime he reaches the water and extra points from the gems.
